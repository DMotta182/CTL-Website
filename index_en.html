Our Services

OUR SERVICES

Custom toll manufacture


Specialized in toll manufacturing of chemical products for crop, warranting customer satisfaction by providing a reliable service, expertise, efficient response time, flexibility to accommodate to customer needs, lower cost for development of new products.

CTL provides repackaging service at the plant, for both liquid and solid products in a wide range. CTL is committed to innovate our services, and we offer MOBILE PACKAGING UNITS. We come to your site and provide certified personnel, repackaging, and filling machines.

International logistics operator


Storage exempt of duties for up to two years. Re-destination of finished goods or manufactured goods to any foreign country.

We provide storage service in Free Trade Zone (ZOLIC) and Free Zone, which allows importing goods under suspension of taxes for marketing or re-export in the same state.

Additionally, if you need manufacturing, we can transport the goods under the temporary admission regime to be manufactured and re-export it.

ABOUT US

CTL is a company mainly dedicated to tolling and manufacturing by contract of Chemical Products.

CTL was created in 2005 and is located in Guatemala, with active and modern ports of entry on both Pacific and Atlantic oceans, turning it into the most important Geographical Platform for export, import and logistics operations for the region and to USA, Canada, Mexico, and Mesoamerica; strongly supported by NAFTA and DR-CAFTA.

Is certified ISO9001.2015, which guarantees the level of quality of its processes and services.
